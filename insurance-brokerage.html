Insurance & Brokerage (Partner-Led)

SH Business Management Ltd provides insurance and brokerage support services to public sector and corporate clients through trusted, FCA-authorised partner organisations.

Our Role

We operate in an advisory, coordination and procurement-support capacity only. All regulated insurance advice, placement and administration is delivered by authorised third-party partners.

Services Supported

  • Insurance needs assessments
  • Risk and compliance coordination
  • Public sector procurement support
  • Contract and framework mobilisation

Partner-Led Delivery

SH Business Management Ltd is not an insurance intermediary and does not provide regulated financial advice. All insurance services are delivered by FCA-regulated partners under separate contractual arrangements.

Clients

  • Local authorities
  • Housing providers
  • Regeneration programmes
  • Corporate and institutional clients
