Our Approach to Fees

S H Business Management Limited operates a transparent and flexible fee structure, aligned with UK market standards for professional consultancy services.

All engagements begin with an initial consultation, followed by a written proposal detailing scope, deliverables, timelines and fees.

Business Consultancy – Indicative Fees

  • Initial strategy consultation: £150 – £300 per hour
  • Short-term consultancy projects: £1,200 – £3,000
  • Mid-sized strategic or operational projects: £3,000 – £8,000
  • Large-scale or transformational engagements: £8,000 – £25,000+
  • Ongoing retainer support: £750 – £3,000 per month

Overseas & UK Investor Advisory – Indicative Fees

Public Sector & Council Consultancy – Indicative Fees

Important Information

The fees listed above are indicative guideline ranges and do not constitute a fixed quotation. Final pricing is confirmed following a scoped consultation and written proposal.

All fees are exclusive of VAT (where applicable).
