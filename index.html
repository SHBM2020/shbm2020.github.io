Who We Are

S H Business Management Limited is an independent business and real estate consultancy supporting all clients with any business or property-related challenges. We work with landlords, investors, developers, SMEs, public bodies and councils across the UK and Northern Ireland.

We have 5 years of active trading supported by 23 years’ experience across business, real estate and community-led development.
