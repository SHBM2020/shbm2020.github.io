Our Role

SH Business Management Ltd does not act as an insurer or insurance broker. All insurance and brokerage services are delivered via regulated partner firms.

What We Provide

  • Insurance requirements mapping for public sector and corporate contracts
  • Coordination with FCA-regulated insurance brokers
  • Contract-specific insurance alignment (PI, PL, EL, D&O)
  • Risk and compliance documentation support
  • Ongoing contract oversight and governance support

Who This Is For

  • Local authorities and public bodies
  • Housing providers and regeneration programmes
  • Corporate and development partners
  • Prime contractors requiring compliant insurance structures

Governance & Compliance

  • Partner-led delivery only
  • FCA-regulated brokers used where required
  • No insurance advice provided directly by SH Business Management Ltd
  • Aligned with public procurement and contract governance standards
